Classic Chili

Deliciously soulful chili that will remind you of mom's homecooking!

A fantastic chili recipe from SoulFoodCooking on YouTube. You'll feel a soothing energy surge through you with each bite.

I stand by this recipe. Easy to make, and even easier to eat.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 2 stalks Celery
  • 14.5oz can Diced Tomatoes
  • 6oz Tomato Paste
  • 1 cup Beef Broth
  • 1 Jalapeno Pepper
  • 15oz can Chili Beans
  • 15oz can Kidney Beans
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Oregano
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tsps Salt
  • 1 Tsps Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. In a frying pan, season the ground beef with 1/2 tsp of salt and black pepper. Cook for 6 minutes or until brown.
  2. In a separate pan, add olive oil and saute the onion, celery, red pepper, green pepper, and jalapeno pepper for 5 minutes. These are diced into small cubes, but feel free to change it as you like.
  3. After 5 minutes, add the ground beef, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and beef broth (fat from ground beef pan).
  4. Add the garlic, cumin, oregano, chili powder, 1/2 tsp salt and black pepper, and mix well. Adjust the sale and pepper to your taste.
  5. Lastly, add the chili and kidney beans, and mix well.
  6. Cover the pot and allow it to simmer on low heat for 2 hours while stirring ocassionally.
  7. After 2 hours, remove from heat and serve.
